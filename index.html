About

We are a Small Business Consultatory specializing in the Cannabis & Hemp Industry by providing Operational Support Along with Web Development & Hosting. We formed out of the Pandemic of 2020 when the Owner was laid off and changed a hobby into a career. Since then, has Obtained all licensing and certifications needed and continued learning about the ever-changing landscape of Cannabis & Web Development to provide the best service possible.
